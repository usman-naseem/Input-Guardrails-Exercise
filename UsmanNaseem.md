# Usman Naseem

Usman Naseem is a Lecturer (~Assistant Professor) in the School of Computing at Macquarie University, Australia. He has a background in both academia and industry, with over 10 years of experience in various technical and leadership roles before entering academia.

## Research Interests

His research in Natural Language Processing (NLP) focuses on three interconnected themes:

*   **Trust and Safety**
*   **LLM Alignment**
*   **NLP for Social Good**

A central focus of his current work is LLM Alignment with Human Preferences, Values, Cultures, and Safety, exploring how large language models can reason more reliably, reflect diverse worldviews, and operate responsibly. He leads the NLP for Social Good Lab - SocialNLP, which develops safe, aligned, and socially impactful language technologies.
